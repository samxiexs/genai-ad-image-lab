Use the source theory's brand concept-image logic as the execution basis. For a functional concept, the image should translate the brand meaning into the product's practical problem-solving value. The product should be presented as a visually understandable answer to a consumption-related problem, so that the viewer can infer what the product helps accomplish, improve, simplify, protect, repair, clean, organize, measure, prepare, support, or make more reliable.

The functional meaning should be grounded in the product itself and in the product's relationship to a plausible use situation.
