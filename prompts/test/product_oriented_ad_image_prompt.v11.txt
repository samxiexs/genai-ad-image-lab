Brand concept definition:
Park et al. define a brand concept as a selected brand meaning based on consumer needs. For this prompt, communicate a functional brand concept. Functional means the product is linked to externally generated consumption needs: solving or preventing a consumption problem, reducing friction, supporting a practical task, or making consumption more reliable.

Primary route of persuasion:
The dominant reading must be functional problem solving, task support, or practical affordance. Viewers should quickly infer what practical job the product helps with. Symbolic identity meaning and experiential stimulation must remain clearly secondary.

1. From the white-background source image, identify what the product visibly is and what visible parts or affordances can be trusted.
